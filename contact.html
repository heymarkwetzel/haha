FAQs

What is Haha Hot Dogs?

Mainly a collection of fun hot dog postcards. There's also a tshirt out there and I'm working on stickers, pins, ornaments, plush, prints, restaurant, and more!

Where are you and how long as this been a thing?

Haha Hot Dogs is based in Chicago and has been a thing for decades or since 2017 depending on when you count "been a thing" from.

Can I sell these postcards in my shop?

It would be an honor! I love when the hot dogs are on the shelf for people to browse.
Please email me with your name, Shop name/URL, and where you're located. I'd love to work with you!

I need to own these! Where can I buy them?

If you're in Chicago - Shop 1021, Wolfbait, and Komoda have cards in stock all year round. You can also buy direct from me at my Etsy store!
If you need something that isn't offered there then feel free to shoot me an email!

What are the postcards printed on?

All postcards are printed on 18pt card stock with a matte coating on the front (image side) and no coating on the back, so they are perfect for writing on. The ink goes on smoothly and does not smear.
I've been partnering with the helpful and knowledgable people at Logan Square's own Rachel Street Press for all postcards.
